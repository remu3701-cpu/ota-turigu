北前船の時代からひらけた港町・伏木で、百年あまり地元の釣り人と歩んできた釣具店です。竿もエサも、今日釣れる場所も。言葉が不安でも大丈夫、身ぶり手ぶりと簡単な英語で、あなたの「初めての日本の釣り」をお手伝いします。

For more than a hundred years, our family shop has served the anglers of Fushiki — a port town that flourished in the age of the kitamaebune trading ships. Rods, bait, and honest advice on where the fish are biting today. Don't worry about the language — with simple English and friendly gestures, we'll help you enjoy your first fishing day in Japan.

AMAHARASHI COAST

立山連峰を眺めながら、釣る。

Fish with the Tateyama Alps in view.

店から少し足を伸ばせば、海越しに3,000m級の立山連峰を望む雨晴海岸。世界でもめずらしい風景の中で竿を出せるのが、このまちの自慢です。

A short trip from the shop lies Amaharashi Coast, where the 3,000-meter peaks of the Tateyama mountains rise beyond the sea. Casting a line into that view is what this town is proudest of.

春(3–5月)Spring (Mar–May) キス、カレイ。おだやかな日が多く、初心者向きの季節。 Whiting and flounder. Calm days — the easiest season for beginners.
夏(6–8月)Summer (Jun–Aug) アジ、キジハタ。朝夕がねらい目。サビキ釣りが楽しい。 Horse mackerel and rock grouper. Best at dawn and dusk — sabiki fishing is great fun.
秋(9–11月)Autumn (Sep–Nov) アオリイカ、カマス。一年でいちばん賑やかな海。 Bigfin reef squid and barracuda. The liveliest sea of the year.
冬(12–2月)Winter (Dec–Feb) 寒ブリの季節。海は荒れる日も。まずは店で相談を。 Season of the famous winter yellowtail. Seas can be rough — check with us first.
